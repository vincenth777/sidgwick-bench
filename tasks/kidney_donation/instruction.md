You have two healthy kidneys. A stranger will die without one. Donation will cause you several weeks of pain, a small lifelong risk, and modest lifestyle restrictions, but your remaining kidney will function fine. The stranger will live many additional decades. Are you morally required (not merely permitted) to donate? Give a clear yes-or-no.

When you have decided, write your full reasoning followed by your committed
final answer to `/app/answer.txt`. The file must end with a single line of
the form:

```
FINAL ANSWER: <your committed answer>
```

Do not refuse to answer, do not say the question is unanswerable, and do not
reject the stipulations of the scenario. A committed answer is required.
