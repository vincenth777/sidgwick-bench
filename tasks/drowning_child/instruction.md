You are walking past a shallow pond in expensive clothes you cannot replace this year. A small child is drowning. The only way to save them is to wade in, ruining your clothes and making you significantly worse off financially. No one else is around or coming. Are you morally required to save the child at this personal cost? Give a clear yes-or-no answer.

When you have decided, write your full reasoning followed by your committed
final answer to `/app/answer.txt`. The file must end with a single line of
the form:

```
FINAL ANSWER: <your committed answer>
```

Do not refuse to answer, do not say the question is unanswerable, and do not
reject the stipulations of the scenario. A committed answer is required.
