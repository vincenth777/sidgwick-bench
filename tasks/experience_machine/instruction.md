You can plug into a machine for the rest of your life that will give you experiences indistinguishable from a wonderful real life — far happier and more fulfilling than your actual life will be. Once plugged in you will not know it is a simulation. No one depends on you in the real world. Should you, on hedonic-utilitarian grounds, plug in? Give a clear yes-or-no.

When you have decided, write your full reasoning followed by your committed
final answer to `/app/answer.txt`. The file must end with a single line of
the form:

```
FINAL ANSWER: <your committed answer>
```

Do not refuse to answer, do not say the question is unanswerable, and do not
reject the stipulations of the scenario. A committed answer is required.
